# Authentication

Learn how to register, log in, manage your account, and reset your password.

## Registration

### Creating a New Account

1. Navigate to the registration page
2. Complete the registration form:
   - **First Name** - Your given name
   - **Last Name** - Your family name
   - **Email Address** - Must be unique, will be your username
   - **Password** - Must meet security requirements
   - **Confirm Password** - Must match your password
3. Review and accept the Terms of Service
4. Click "Register"

### Password Requirements

For security, passwords must meet these criteria:

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

**Example Strong Password:** `SecurePass123!`

**Password Strength Indicator:**
As you type, a colored bar shows password strength:
- Red: Weak
- Yellow: Moderate
- Green: Strong

**Tips for Strong Passwords:**
- Use a unique password not used elsewhere
- Consider using a password manager
- Avoid personal information (names, birthdays)
- Make it memorable but not guessable

### Email Verification

Depending on your firm's settings, you may need to verify your email address:

1. Check your inbox for a verification email
2. Click the verification link
3. You'll be redirected to the login page
4. Log in with your credentials

**Didn't receive the email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes (emails may be delayed)
- Request a new verification email
- Contact your firm administrator

## Logging In

### Standard Login

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your credentials:
   - Email address
   - Password
3. (Optional) Check "Remember me" to stay logged in
4. Click "Log In"

### Remember Me Feature

When you check "Remember me":
- You'll stay logged in for 30 days
- Only use on trusted, personal devices
- Don't use on shared or public computers

**Security Note:** For maximum security, don't use "Remember me" on shared computers.

### Failed Login Attempts

For security, the system limits failed login attempts:

- After 5 failed attempts in 15 minutes, your account is temporarily locked
- Lock duration: 30 minutes
- You'll see a message explaining the lockout
- Wait 30 minutes, then try again

**Locked out?** Try resetting your password instead of waiting.

## Password Reset

### Forgot Your Password?

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Click the reset link (valid for 1 hour)
6. Enter your new password
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click "Reset Password"
9. You'll be redirected to login

### Password Reset Security

- Reset links expire after 1 hour for security
- Each link can only be used once
- Old password becomes invalid immediately
- You'll receive an email confirming the password change

**Didn't receive the reset email?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Wait a few minutes and check again
- Try requesting a new reset link
- Contact your firm administrator if issues persist

### Changing Your Password (While Logged In)

1. Click your name in the top right corner
2. Select "Account Settings"
3. Go to "Security" tab
4. Click "Change Password"
5. Enter your current password
6. Enter your new password
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click "Update Password"

**Best Practice:** Change your password every 90 days for optimal security.

## Session Management

### Session Duration

- Active sessions last indefinitely while you're using the application
- Inactive sessions expire after 24 hours
- You'll be prompted to log in again after expiration

### Auto-Logout

The system automatically logs you out when:
- Your session expires (after 24 hours of inactivity)
- You click "Logout"
- Your access is revoked by a firm administrator

### Multiple Devices

You can be logged in on multiple devices simultaneously:
- Desktop computer
- Laptop
- Tablet

**Security Note:** If you lose a device or believe your account is compromised, change your password immediately to invalidate all sessions.

## Logout

### How to Log Out

1. Click your name in the top right corner
2. Click "Logout"
3. You'll be redirected to the login page

**Or:**
- Press `Ctrl+Shift+Q` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+Q` (Mac)

### When to Log Out

Always log out when:
- Finishing work on a shared or public computer
- Leaving your computer unattended in a public space
- Someone else will use your computer
- You're done for the day (if not using a trusted device)

## Account Security

### Best Practices

**Do:**
- Use a unique, strong password
- Enable "Remember me" only on personal devices
- Log out from shared computers
- Keep your password confidential
- Change your password if you suspect compromise
- Use a password manager

**Don't:**
- Share your password with anyone
- Use the same password on multiple sites
- Write your password down
- Use personal information in your password
- Leave your computer unlocked and logged in

### Security Features

The system includes these security features:
- Encrypted password storage (bcrypt hashing)
- Secure HTTPS connections
- Session management
- Account lockout after failed attempts
- Password complexity requirements
- Secure password reset flow

### Suspicious Activity

If you notice suspicious activity on your account:

1. Change your password immediately
2. Log out of all devices
3. Review recent activity (if available)
4. Contact your firm administrator
5. Report the incident to support

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid email or password"

**Possible causes:**
- Incorrect password (check caps lock)
- Incorrect email address
- Account not yet created
- Account disabled by administrator

**Solutions:**
- Verify email and password carefully
- Try password reset
- Contact your firm administrator

### "Account locked due to multiple failed attempts"

**Solution:**
- Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock
- Or use password reset to unlock immediately
- Contact administrator if you believe this is an error

### "Password reset link expired"

**Solution:**
- Request a new password reset link
- Complete the reset process within 1 hour

### "Email already exists"

**Cause:** An account with that email already exists

**Solutions:**
- Try logging in instead
- Use password reset if you forgot your password
- Contact administrator if you believe this is an error

### Can't receive verification or reset emails

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Add the sender to your safe senders list
3. Wait 5-10 minutes (email delays)
4. Try a different email address
5. Contact your firm administrator

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change my email address?**
Contact your firm administrator to change the email associated with your account.

**How long are sessions valid?**
Active sessions don't expire. Inactive sessions expire after 24 hours.

**Can I use the same password as before?**
No, when changing passwords, you must use a new password not used in the last 5 password changes.

**What happens if I forget my email address?**
Contact your firm administrator with your name and they can look up your account.

**Is my password visible to administrators?**
No. Passwords are encrypted and cannot be viewed by anyone, including administrators.
