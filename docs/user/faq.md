# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Quick answers to common questions about the Demand Letter Generator.

## General Questions

**What is the Demand Letter Generator?**
An AI-powered tool that helps legal professionals create demand letters by analyzing source documents and generating customized letters based on templates.

**Who can use this system?**
Legal professionals (attorneys, paralegals) at law firms with active subscriptions.

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with firm-level isolation ensuring your documents and letters are only accessible to your firm.

**How much does it cost?**
Contact your firm administrator for pricing information. Costs typically include subscription fees plus usage-based charges for AI processing.

**Is there a mobile app?**
Not yet. The web application works on mobile browsers but is optimized for desktop use.

## Account and Authentication

**How do I create an account?**
You must be invited by a firm administrator. They'll send an invitation email with a registration link.

**I forgot my password. What do I do?**
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the reset instructions sent to your email.

**Can I change my email address?**
Contact your firm administrator to update the email associated with your account.

**How often should I change my password?**
We recommend changing passwords every 90 days for optimal security.

**Can I use the same account on multiple devices?**
Yes. Log in from any device with your credentials.

## Documents

**What file formats are supported?**
PDF (.pdf), Microsoft Word (.docx), and plain text (.txt).

**How large can uploaded documents be?**
Maximum 50MB per file.

**How many documents can I upload?**
There's no strict limit, but practical storage limits apply per firm. Contact your administrator for details.

**How long are documents stored?**
Indefinitely unless you delete them or your subscription ends.

**Can I edit uploaded documents?**
No. To update content, upload a new version. You can edit metadata (title, description, tags).

**What happens to deleted documents?**
Deletion is permanent. The document is removed from storage and cannot be recovered.

## Templates

**Who can create templates?**
Only firm administrators can create and edit templates.

**Can I suggest template changes?**
Yes. Contact your firm administrator with suggested improvements.

**How many templates can we have?**
Unlimited templates per firm.

**Can templates be shared between firms?**
No. Templates are firm-specific for confidentiality reasons.

**What if a template variable doesn't have data?**
The variable will appear as blank or show a default value in the generated letter. You can manually fill it in.

## Demand Letter Generation

**How long does document analysis take?**
Typically 20-30 seconds for a 10-page document. Larger documents may take up to 60 seconds.

**How accurate is the AI?**
The AI is highly accurate for extracting standard information like names, dates, and damages. Always review and verify before generating a letter.

**Can I edit the AI's analysis?**
Yes. You can edit, add, or remove any extracted facts before generating your letter.

**How long does letter generation take?**
Initial generation takes 5-10 seconds. Refinements take similar time.

**How many times can I refine a letter?**
Unlimited refinements, though 2-3 typically achieves desired results.

**Can I manually edit generated letters?**
Yes. Full text editing is available in the letter editor.

**What if the AI makes a mistake?**
You can manually correct any part of the letter or request a refinement with specific instructions.

**Do refinements cost extra?**
Each refinement uses AI tokens, which may have cost implications. Check with your administrator about usage limits.

## Collaboration

**How many people can edit a letter simultaneously?**
Up to 10 users can collaborate on the same letter in real-time.

**Can I work offline?**
You can view and edit offline, but changes won't sync until you're back online.

**Can I see who made what changes?**
Yes. The version history shows who made each change and when.

**Can I collaborate with opposing counsel?**
No. Collaboration is limited to users within your firm.

**What happens if two people edit the same text?**
Changes merge automatically using conflict-resolution technology. The latest edit takes precedence.

## Export and Delivery

**Should I export to Word or PDF?**
Use Word if you need to make further edits. Use PDF for final delivery to opposing counsel.

**Can I edit a PDF after export?**
Not directly. Export to Word, edit there, then convert to PDF.

**Why doesn't my firm letterhead appear?**
Your administrator must configure firm branding in settings.

**Can I email directly from the system?**
Not currently. Export the file and attach it to your email client.

**Are exports tracked?**
Yes. All exports are logged with timestamp and user information.

## Technical Issues

**The system is running slowly. What should I do?**
Try refreshing your browser, clearing cache, or using a different browser. Contact support if issues persist.

**I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?**
Check the [Troubleshooting Guide](./troubleshooting.md) for common errors and solutions.

**Can I use Internet Explorer?**
No. Use modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest 2 versions).

**Do I need to install anything?**
No. The system runs entirely in your web browser.

**What if I lose my internet connection while working?**
Your work is auto-saved every 30 seconds. Changes made offline will sync when you reconnect.

## Data and Privacy

**Who can see my documents and letters?**

**Is data backed up?**

**What happens to my data if I leave the firm?**
Your account is deactivated but your work remains accessible to the firm.
