# Getting Started Guide

Welcome to the Demand Letter Generator! This guide will walk you through creating your first demand letter in under 10 minutes.

## Overview

The Demand Letter Generator is an AI-powered tool that helps legal professionals create professional demand letters quickly and efficiently. The system:

1. Analyzes your source documents (incident reports, medical records, etc.)
2. Extracts key facts and damages
3. Generates a demand letter based on your firm's templates
4. Allows iterative refinement and collaboration
5. Exports to Word or PDF for final delivery

## Your First Demand Letter

### Step 1: Create an Account (2 minutes)

1. Navigate to the registration page
2. Enter your information:
   - First and last name
   - Email address
   - Secure password (8+ characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
3. Accept the terms of service
4. Click "Register"
5. Check your email for a verification link (if required)

**Tip:** Use a strong, unique password. Our system requires at least 8 characters with a mix of character types.

### Step 2: Log In (1 minute)

1. Visit the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Log In"
4. You'll be directed to your dashboard

**Tip:** Check "Remember me" to stay logged in on trusted devices.

### Step 3: Upload Source Documents (2 minutes)

1. Navigate to "Documents" from the sidebar
2. Click "Upload Document" or drag and drop files
3. Supported formats: PDF, Word (.docx), Text (.txt)
4. Add up to 10 documents (50MB each)
5. Give each document a descriptive title
6. Click "Upload"

**What to upload:**
- Incident reports
- Medical records
- Repair estimates
- Correspondence
- Photos or evidence summaries
- Any relevant documentation

**Tip:** Clear, well-organized source documents lead to better results. Ensure all documents are legible.

### Step 4: Create a New Demand Letter (1 minute)

1. Navigate to "Demand Letters" from the sidebar
2. Click "New Demand Letter"
3. Fill in basic information:
   - Case name or identifier
   - Select the template to use
   - Choose source documents to analyze
4. Click "Create"

The system will begin analyzing your documents automatically.

### Step 5: Review AI Analysis (2 minutes)

Once analysis completes (typically 20-30 seconds):

1. Review the "Analysis" panel on the left
2. Check extracted information:
   - Parties involved (plaintiff, defendant)
   - Incident date and location
   - Injuries and damages
   - Relevant facts
3. Edit any incorrect or missing information
4. Click "Approve Analysis" or "Refine Analysis" with instructions

**Tip:** The more accurate your analysis data, the better your generated letter will be.

### Step 6: Generate the Letter (1 minute)

1. Click "Generate Letter" button
2. The AI will create a draft using your template and analyzed facts
3. Generation takes 5-10 seconds
4. Review the generated letter in the editor

### Step 7: Refine and Finalize (1 minute)

1. Read through the generated demand letter
2. Make direct edits in the editor, or
3. Use "Refine Letter" to request AI revisions:
   - "Make the tone more aggressive"
   - "Add more detail about the medical injuries"
   - "Emphasize the negligence aspect"
4. Click "Approve" when satisfied

**Tip:** You can iterate multiple times. Each refinement improves the letter.

### Step 8: Export Your Letter

1. Click the "Export" button
2. Choose your format:
   - **Word (.docx)** - for further editing
   - **PDF** - for final delivery
3. Download the file
4. Your letter is ready to send!

## Common Questions

**How long does document analysis take?**
Typically 20-30 seconds for a 10-page document. Larger documents may take up to a minute.

**How accurate is the AI?**
The AI is highly accurate for extracting standard information (names, dates, injuries). Always review and verify the analysis before generating a letter.

**Can I edit templates?**
Yes! Firm administrators can create and edit templates. See [Templates Guide](./templates.md).

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. All documents are encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to your firm only. See our security documentation for details.

**How many iterations can I do?**
You can refine a letter as many times as needed. Each iteration uses AI tokens, which may have cost implications for your firm.
